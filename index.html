The Beautiful Game

Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport involving two teams of 11 players each. The game is played on an oval field with a 22-yard pitch at the center. The objective is to score more runs than the opposing team. Runs are scored by hitting the ball and running between wickets or by hitting boundaries. The team with the most runs wins the match7
